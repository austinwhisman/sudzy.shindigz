What is a Foam Party?

A foam party is just what it sounds like – an event where you can play, dance, and party in several feet of bubbly suds. Created by a foam machine, the foam is light, fluffy, and mess-free, unlike the sticky, wet consistency of soap bubbles. Guests can enjoy the foam on the dance floor or take part in special foam activities. Foam parties are perfect for guests of all ages and are popular for school and daycare events.

While foam parties are often associated with kids, they are also incredibly fun for adults and college students. We have hosted many unforgettable adult and college foam parties, proving that foam fun is truly for everyone!
